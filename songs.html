My EuroPop Top 12

A recopilation of my 12 favourite Eurovision Songs of all times

1. Euphoria

Loreen - Sweden 2012

The winner of the winners, Loreen won the Contest back in 2012 in Bakú with this Euro Hit that 9 years later it stills as the favourite of many fans. This song became the 5th winning for the country, now with 6, Sweden is the second country with more winnings after Ireland.

3. Dime

Beth - Spain 2003

Even she didn't like the song, Dime is one of the most representatives songs of Spain in the Contest an set a very important moment for all the Spaniard with an 8th position. In my humble opinion, the one of best spanish representant that the country ever had.

5. Diva

Dana International - Israel 1998

Maria, Victoria, Aphrodita and Cleopatra, the biggest Divas in the World Herstory, but the biggest diva in Eurovision is Dana. Winning in 1998 it became the second winning for Israel. The song became instantly an anthem for all the eurofans and nowadays it stills a jam in all the EuroParties.

7. Fuego

Eleni Foureira - Cyprus 2018

Greek girl representing Cyprus with the ultimate latino hit? Thats Eleni with Fuego. She lit up all the homes worldwide with her fire and killed in the Lisbon Stage in 2018. Sadly she ended second but was the real winner for many. A tip for all of you, warm up before shacking all the hair as she does.

9. Rhythm Inside

Eloïc Nottet - Belgium 2015

With his peculiar rhythm Eloïc departed to Vienna in 2015 as one of the favourites to win the contest but in the end he finishined 4th but he made dance all Europe with his distinctive beat.

11. Golden Boy

Nadav Guedj - Israel 2015

He came for the gold and he left with a magnific 9th position. Another of the Biggest Hits in the Contest history we all remember his tickly dances moves on the 2015 stage. Of course we want you to show is Tel Aviv!

2. Arcade

Duncan Lawrence - The Netherlands 2019

The current winning song ans the longest that ever has held this title due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Arcade became the second winning for The Netherlands after 40 years trying. This song got popular world wide thanks to a tiktok trend that used it.

4. Soldi

Mahmood - Italy 2019

Soldi clap clap, finished 2nd in the 2019 contest in Lisbon and it was the winner of the San Remo Festival in the same year, as all of the italians representatives in the contest. It became the fresh air that Italy needed in Eurovision and it went back home with one of the highests scores that the country ever recived.

6. Quedate Conmigo

Pastora Soler - Spain 2012

We all want to Stay with Her so it was in Baku in 2012. Finishing in 10th position, one of the best for Spain in the past decades. That high pitch left all Europe gaping.

8. Waterloo

ABBA - Sweeden 1974

Let's go back in time. In 1974 a small swedish group called won the contest, maybe you've heard Waterloo, the winning song, in a couple indie movies like Mamma Mia. Just kidding, who has not danced to the rhythm of Waterloo or any ABBA song. They are the ultimate Euro Group and putted Sweden and its musical talent up to the world. We promise we Will love you Forever More!

10. Grande Amore

Il Volo - Italy 2015

We all fell in love with them. Representing Italy at the 2015 ESC Il Volo try to won it with an italian ballad but they didnt succeed. But in the end we all have a Grande Amore in them.

12. Rise like a Phoenix

Conchita Wurst - Austria 2014

THE ULTIMATE BALLAD, the Queen of Eurovision. Conchita made Herstory in the Show with a show-stopping performance in Copenhague Stage that gave Austria his second ever victory. She rised from the ashes to become one of the most influencial LGTBQ+ personalities at the world. You Are Unstoppable.
