Someone told me you want to know more about me so here we go!

Im Mark, from Catalunya and I'm 20 years old. I'm a Geography student at the Rovira Virgili Univeristy in Vila-Seca, Tarragona. Apart of this, I am a HUGE Eurovision fan.

Before being the big fan I am nowadays, I remember watching some of the Grand Finals on TV and i can not forget about the Grand Final from 2007 when Lordi won. But i started enjoying the ESC in the full way when i was 9 years old with Lena winning in Oslo with Satellite. That year my view of Eurovision changed completly and I started living the full experience. Since then every year I try to catch up with all the National Finals that take place from January until mid March and I'm expecting the end of April when the first delegations arrive to the host city to start rehersing and the fisrts stagings come alive.
