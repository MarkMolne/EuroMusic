What is the Eurovision Song Contest?

A recap about ESC's history

The Eurovision Song Contest was created as an idea to promote cooperation between European countries through music after the Second World War. In April 1956 the first ever ESC was held in Lugano, Switzerland with 7 countries participating. The winner was also the host country represented by Lys Assia with the song Refrain. Since then, 64 Contests have taken place in all over Europe until 2020 when the show was cancelled due to COVID-19. If you want to lear more about Eurovision visit the Oficial Eurovision Webpage.
